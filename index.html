F1 Cost-Cap Analytics: Forecasting & Predictive Modeling

MS Capstone · Forecasting & ML
Question
After F1 capped every team's budget, does spending money efficiently still predict winning, or does raw spend matter less than people think?
What I did
I owned the forecasting and predictive-modeling workstreams: ARIMAX time-series forecasting and a Random Forest model across 11 seasons, on a shared SQL/Tableau data layer my team assembled.
Result
Forecast baseline MAPE of 5.03%; the Random Forest model outperformed a deep neural network; the analysis linked financial efficiency to on-track performance.
Tools: R, Python (scikit-learn), Tableau, SQL

Sales Pipeline & Revenue Operations Analysis

Sales Operations · Pipeline, Win Rates, Forecasting
Question
Acting as the sales-ops analyst for a SaaS team: is the pipeline healthy, where do deals leak, and what should leadership do about it?
What I did
Analyzed 560 opportunities end to end: pipeline health by stage, win rates by rep, region, and source, sales-cycle comparison, and a stage-weighted revenue forecast.
Decision
Found the leak is qualification quality (not speed), flagged a 13-point regional win-rate gap, and recommended shifting effort to higher-converting lead sources; forecast $2.32M weighted from $4.97M open pipeline.
